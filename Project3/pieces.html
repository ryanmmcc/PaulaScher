PIECES

She has developed identity and branding systems, promotional materials, environmental graphics, packaging, and publication design for a broad range of clients that includes Microsoft, Adobe, Coca-Cola, Shake Shack, the Museum of Modern Art, and many others.


One of Paula's greatest achievements was being the first designer to create a new identity and different promotional graphics for The Public Theater in 1994. This included creating the logo, large-scale billboards, posters, and even stationary.


Scher began painting these colorful typographic maps of the world in 1990. They consisted of the worlds continents, countries, islands, streets and neighborhoods. Paula released her book MAPS in 2010 where the 39 paintings are collected for the first time in.
